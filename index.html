Our Story

We’re a small, family-run team that always dreamed of opening a café. Starting with a food cart lets us share our recipes, learn from the community, and grow toward a future brick‑and‑mortar.

Named after our cat Azula, Zula's Tea & Coffee is built on love, creativity, and a passion for sharing moments that feel like home.

Thank you for being part of our journey!
